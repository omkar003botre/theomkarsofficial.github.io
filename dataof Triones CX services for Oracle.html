Triones CX services for Oracle

Triones Solution

Triones enables customer experience (CX) transformation by providing a rich portfolio of solutions and services in the Oracle ecosystem.
Triones CX Services for Oracle helps enterprises reimagine customer experiences, matching CX delivery to the expectations of
a digital-age customer. However, every enterprise brings unique requirements and a differentiated approach,
requiring custom-implementation of the Oracle technology stack.
Triones supports and realigns Oracle CX implementations in a variety of areas:

  1. CX transformation services for digital front office, applying cutting-edge technologies such as AI-ML, AR-VR,
    and IoT.

  2. CX digital experience services, helping design an intelligent CRM based on customer and employee UX assessment.

  3. CX modernization services, moving on-premise applications to the cloud through Triones Machine First Delivery
    ModelTM.

  4. CX industry solutions, leveraging futuristic technology such as omnichannel CX for commerce and video-assisted
    CRM for marketing and sales.

Benefits

  1. Enhance revenue by easing customer acquisition and cross-selling / up-selling opportunities.
  2. Accelerate time-to-market via agile, scalable Oracle cloud framework with pre-integrations.
  3. Drive a smart enterprise using analytics, for unique and repeatable experiences.
  4. Ensure competitive differentiation through gamification, digital commerce, and more.
